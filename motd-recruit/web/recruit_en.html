Admin recruitment

Do you enjoy playing on our CS:GO servers? Do you want to be part of our team, actively maintain order on servers and help to improve them? Submit your application and maybe you will get chance to show your skills!

Minimal requirements

  • Knowledge of the game: minimum 300 played hours on Steam.
  • Activity on our servers: minimum 50 played hours on our CSGO servers. (you can check that at stats.ezpz.cz – find yourself and look at "Connection Time")
  • Representavive behaviour and keeping the rules: if you join us, you will represent not only yourself but the whole team. That is why it is important that we can relay on you.
  • Creativity and free time: we will need you as a real problem solver and creator of new things, not just nickname in the admin list.

Job description

  • Maintaining order on the server according to the rules and ensure the continuity of the game.
  • Analysis of the game on the servers and comunication with management about what should be improved.
  • Solving of the problems and complaints of players.
  • Creation of activities (contests...) that could improve the traffic or the presige of the portal.

What should be in your application

  • Basic information about yourself
  • Which specific server / group are you interested in
  • Previous experience with admin position, if exists
  • Why are you applying for the position
  • Your other abilities which could be beneficial for our portal (programming e.g. HTML, PHP, objective programming languages, marketing, etc.)
  • Contact (especially the reference to your Steam profile)
